About

Overthrone is a co-operative turn based game in which you and a friend play as Queen Freydis and King Ragnar. Tricked by the devilish Lord of the Wood, you have been asleep for a hundred years, only to be awakened by a puny crownling seeking to use your power for their own gain. It is time to arise, c​all on your elemental patrons, and remind the kingdom of your names!

  • Choose different skills each run as your powers reawaken after your lon​g slumber. 
  • Destroy your enemies in tactical, ro​gue-like combat. 
  • Explore your dominion and root out every last would-be usurper you can find. 

Unleash your inner villain and claim your vengeance!
